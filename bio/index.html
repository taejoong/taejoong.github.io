Bio

Taejoong (Tijay) Chung is an Assistant Professor at the Computer Science department in B. Thomas Golisano College of Computing and Information Sciences at the Rochester Institute of Technology. Before joining RIT, he spent 3 years at Northeastern university working with Prof. Alan Mislove and David Choffnes. He received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and Engineering from Seoul National University in 2015. His work focuses on Internet security, privacy implications, and Internet measurement. He received the IRTF Applied Networking Research Prize (2019), ACM IMC Distinguished Paper Award (2019), USENIX Security Distinguished Paper Award (2017), and Best Paper Award at IEEE Computer Society (ComSoc) Seoul Chapter (2010).
