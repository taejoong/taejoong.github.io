ACM Internet Measurement Conference 2019

The 2019 Internet Measurement Conference (IMC) is a three-day event focusing on Internet measurement and analysis. The conference is sponsored by ACM SIGCOMM. IMC 2019 is the 18th in a series of highly successful Internet Measurement Workshops and Conferences.

The ACM IMC 2019 conference will be held in Amsterdam, Netherlands on October 21 - 23, 2019.
